Stephanie Dang Graphic Designer | Digital Artist

Hi, I'm Stéphanie Dang.


I am a graphic designer based in Montréal, Canada, with a BFA in Computation Arts at Concordia University and a DEC in Multimedia Integration from Collège de Maisonneuve.

My specialization lies in poster creation and motion design, earning me the 2nd place at the Exposition d’Affiches et de Concept de Jeux Vidéos edition 2017, organized by Collège de Maisonneuve. These skills have led me to intern at various organizations such as Simbioz, GFN Productions, and BetterSleep. In these roles, I crafted wireframes and developed printed and digital advertising creatives, including posters and videos.
