Takeback

Over the course of a four-week game jam, I took on the role of overseeing art direction and creating various 2D assets, including UI elements, character designs, background visuals, and a cut scene. This marked my initial exploration into tilesets and tilemaps, and I thoroughly enjoyed the learning process of achieving seamless and repeatable designs. The game revolved around the theme of revenge, with the added challenge of incorporating no text.

Platformer game where the player has to infiltrate the Castle at night in order to steal back gold and treasure that is rightfully theirs while also avoiding traps.
