Gachapyon

In this project, I set out to create a game entirely on my own, presenting challenges both in terms of artistic creativity and programming skills. I utilized technologies such as HTML3/CSS5, JavaScript, JQuery/JQueryUI, LocalStorage, JSON, and P5.js for programming, while Adobe Illustrator served as the primary software for all illustrations. This remains one of my favorite projects to date, given the complete autonomy I had, allowing me to shape it exactly as I envisioned.

You play as a cat that enjoys collecting toys from capsule and gacha games. To sustain your hobby, you need to work some part-time job. The goal is to collect all the items!
